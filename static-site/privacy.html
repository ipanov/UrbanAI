Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Privacy-First Approach

UrbanAI is designed with privacy at its core. We use OAuth-only authentication and store no personal identifiable information (PII). Your privacy is not just protected—it's built into the architecture of our platform.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Authentication Data

When you sign in using OAuth providers (Google, Microsoft), we receive:

  • Your unique OAuth identifier (not your email or name)
  • Authentication tokens (stored securely and encrypted)
  • No personal profile information is stored on our servers

1.2 Issue Reports

When you submit urban issue reports, we collect:

  • Issue description and category
  • Location data (if you choose to share it)
  • Photos or attachments you voluntarily upload
  • Timestamp of the report
  • No connection to your personal identity

1.3 Technical Data

For service functionality and security:

  • Session tokens (temporary, automatically expire)
  • Basic usage analytics (anonymized)
  • Error logs (no personal data included)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Service Provision: Enabling you to report and track urban issues
  • Municipal Coordination: Forwarding anonymous reports to relevant authorities
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance functionality
  • Security: Preventing abuse and maintaining service integrity

We never:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Share personal information with municipal authorities
  • Track your personal behavior across other websites

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Anonymous Issue Reports

Issue reports are shared with municipal authorities in completely anonymous form, containing only:

  • Issue description and location
  • Photos or attachments (if provided)
  • Report timestamp
  • No identifying information about the reporter

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, but given our minimal data collection, there is little personal information that could be disclosed.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Location

All data is stored in Azure data centers within the European Union, ensuring GDPR compliance.

4.2 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Encrypted storage using Azure Security standards
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and audit logging
  • Automatic data retention policies

4.3 Data Retention

  • Authentication tokens: Expire automatically (typically 24 hours)
  • Issue reports: Retained for municipal record-keeping (typically 7 years)
  • Usage analytics: Aggregated and anonymized after 90 days

5. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a user in the European Union, you have the following rights:

5.1 Right to Access

Request information about what data we hold about you (which is minimal due to our privacy-first design).

5.2 Right to Rectification

Request correction of any inaccurate data (limited applicability due to our OAuth-only approach).

5.3 Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your data. Note that issue reports may be retained for legal municipal record-keeping requirements.

5.4 Right to Data Portability

Request export of your data in a machine-readable format.

5.5 Right to Object

Object to processing of your data for specific purposes.

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@urbanai.site

Response time: Within 30 days as required by GDPR

6. Cookies and Tracking

6.1 Essential Cookies

We use only essential cookies necessary for the service to function:

  • Authentication session cookies
  • Security tokens
  • User preference settings

6.2 No Tracking Cookies

We do not use:

  • Advertising cookies
  • Social media tracking
  • Cross-site tracking
  • Analytics cookies that identify individuals

7. Third-Party Services

7.1 OAuth Providers

We integrate with Google and Microsoft for authentication. Their privacy policies apply to the authentication process:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Microsoft Privacy Statement

7.2 Azure Cloud Services

Our infrastructure is hosted on Microsoft Azure, which maintains ISO 27001 and SOC 2 compliance.

8. Children's Privacy

UrbanAI is not intended for use by children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

All data processing occurs within the European Union. In the rare case of international transfers, we ensure adequate protection through:

  • Adequacy decisions by the European Commission
  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Other approved transfer mechanisms

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We will notify users through the application
  • The updated policy will be posted on this page
  • Significant changes will require explicit consent
  • The "Last updated" date will be revised

11. Contact Information

Email: dpo@urbanai.site

Supervisory Authority

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe your privacy rights have been violated.

12. Commitment to Privacy

UrbanAI is committed to being a privacy-first platform. Our architecture is designed to minimize data collection while maximizing utility for urban issue reporting. We believe privacy and functionality can coexist, and we've built our platform to prove it.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're here to help and committed to transparency in all our privacy practices.
