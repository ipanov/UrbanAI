Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report an urban issue?

Click "Get Started" to access our platform, sign in with your Google or Microsoft account, and follow the guided process to report issues with photos and location data.

Is my personal information stored?

No. UrbanAI uses OAuth authentication and stores no personal identifiable information. All reports are completely anonymous and GDPR compliant.

Which municipalities use UrbanAI?

We're expanding across Europe and globally. Contact partnerships@urbanai.site to discuss implementation for your city or municipality.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes! Our native iOS and Android apps are now available on the App Store and Google Play, offering the full UrbanAI experience optimized for mobile.

How does AI help with issue reporting?

Our multi-agent AI system analyzes reports to categorize issues, discover relevant regulations, assess viability, generate professional documentation, and route to appropriate authorities.

What types of issues can I report?

Construction violations, environmental concerns, safety issues, municipal infrastructure problems, zoning violations, and more. Environmental issues are always free to report.

How much does UrbanAI cost?

We offer a free tier with basic reporting, and paid plans starting at €9.99/month for unlimited reports and professional documentation. Check our pricing page for details.

Can I get help implementing UrbanAI?

Absolutely! We offer full implementation support, training, and custom integration services for municipalities and enterprise customers.
