Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report an urban issue?

Simply click "Get Started" to access the web app, sign in with your Google or Microsoft account, and follow the guided process to report issues with photos and location data.

Is my personal information stored?

No. UrbanAI uses OAuth authentication and stores no personal identifiable information. All reports are completely anonymous.

Which municipalities use UrbanAI?

We're currently in the pilot phase with select European municipalities. Contact us at partnerships@urbanai.site to discuss implementation for your city.

Is there a mobile app?

We currently offer a responsive web app that works great on mobile devices. Native iOS and Android apps are coming soon.

How does AI help with issue reporting?

Our AI analyzes your reports to categorize issues, suggest relevant regulations, assess priority levels, and route reports to the appropriate municipal departments.

Is UrbanAI GDPR compliant?

Yes, UrbanAI is fully GDPR compliant. All data is stored in EU data centers, and we follow strict privacy-first principles with minimal data collection.

How do I track my reported issues?

After reporting an issue, you'll receive a tracking reference. You can check the status anytime through the web app or receive email updates if you choose to provide an email address.

Can I report issues anonymously?

Absolutely. All reports are anonymous by design. We don't store or share any personal information with municipal authorities.
