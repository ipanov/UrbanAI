Our Story

It Started With a Fence

Our founder faced a simple problem: a neighbor's fence that seemed too low according to municipal regulations. What should have been a straightforward issue turned into weeks of research through complex municipal codes, building regulations, and legal frameworks.

After navigating multiple government websites, buried PDFs, and conflicting information, we realized that this shouldn't be so hard. Citizens shouldn't need to become legal experts to report legitimate urban issues.

That's when the idea for UrbanAI was born: what if AI could do all that research automatically? What if every citizen could have access to the same level of professional analysis that lawyers and civil engineers provide?

Today, UrbanAI transforms that weeks-long research process into a few minutes of AI-powered analysis, giving every citizen the tools to create meaningful change in their community.
