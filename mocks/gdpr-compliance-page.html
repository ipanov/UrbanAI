Privacy & Data Protection

Your privacy rights and how we protect your data

Last updated: January 2024

Your GDPR Rights

  • The right to be informed - You have the right to know how your personal data is being used
  • The right of access - You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to rectification - You can request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • The right to erasure - You can request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances
  • The right to restrict processing - You can request limitation of how your data is used
  • The right to data portability - You can obtain and reuse your data across different services
  • The right to object - You can object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Rights in relation to automated decision making - You can request human intervention in automated decisions

What Data We Collect

UrbanAI operates on a privacy-first principle with our OAuth-only authentication approach. We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our services:

Zero PII Storage - We never store personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers. Your identity remains with your chosen authentication provider.

We only store:

  • Anonymous case identifiers and report data
  • Timestamps for report creation and updates
  • Location data associated with municipal issues (not personal locations)
  • Photographs and descriptions of reported issues
  • Technical logs for service improvement and security

All data is encrypted at rest and in transit, ensuring your information remains secure.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance - To provide the UrbanAI service you've requested
  • Legitimate interests - To improve our service, ensure security, and prevent abuse
  • Legal compliance - To meet our obligations under applicable laws

Our legitimate interests include maintaining the security and integrity of our platform, improving user experience, and ensuring compliance with municipal reporting requirements. We always balance these interests against your privacy rights.

Your Data Rights & Choices

You maintain full control over your data:

Account Deletion - You can request complete deletion of your account and associated data at any time through our support portal.

Data Access - Request a complete export of your data in machine-readable format through your account settings.

Opt-Out - Disable analytics tracking and marketing communications at any time in your preferences.

The account deletion process:

  1. Submit deletion request through the support portal
  2. Verification email sent to confirm your identity
  3. Data removal completed within 30 days
  4. Confirmation email sent upon completion
